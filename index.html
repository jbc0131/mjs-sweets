~ good things to know ~

Sweet little questions, answered.

Everything you might be wondering before you order. Got something else? Just text!

What's the flavor profile? +
So glad you asked! Both the cookie and the icing are made completely from scratch with our signature almond flavor profile. It's that warm, bakery-style taste that keeps people coming back for "just one more." Pillowy soft, never dry, with icing that sets perfectly so it's beautiful and delicious.
Are they nut free? +
Yes, we've got you covered! Our lemon and vanilla cookies are 100% nut free and just as delicious. Just let me know when you order and I'll make sure your set is made nut free from start to finish. (Heads up: my standard almond flavor does contain nuts, so always mention any allergies when you reach out!)
When can I order seasonal sets? +
Each seasonal set follows the same easy timeline:

🗓️ 4 weeks before the holiday: ordering opens (this is the sweet spot to grab yours!)
📍 3 days before the holiday: orders close so I have time to make everything beautifully

So if you're eyeing the Mother's Day Box, mark your calendar, and don't wait too long, since seasonal sets are limited and often sell out before the deadline!
How far in advance should I place a custom order? +
For custom designs, please give me at least 2 weeks. Earlier is even better, especially during busy seasons (graduation, Mother's Day, Christmas). The more lead time you give, the more dialed-in your design will be! Need something rushed? Text me anyway, sometimes I can squeeze it in.
Where do I pick up my order? +
Pickup is easy and convenient right here in Madisonville, Louisiana! I'll send you the exact address and a pickup window once your order is confirmed. I happily serve the entire Northshore: Mandeville, Covington, Hammond, Folsom, Abita Springs, Ponchatoula, and beyond.
What's your minimum order? +
Custom orders start at 1 dozen. Need a smaller treat? Keep an eye on my seasonal sets, they're perfect for gifting without committing to a big order!
